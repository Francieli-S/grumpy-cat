Grumpy Cat

This game is inspired by those grumpy but super funny cats who like to push things and watch them fall just for fun and to annoy us, the humans!

The goal is:

  • Collect delicious treats: score +1
  • Avoid toxic chocolates, otherwise: score -1
  • Push cups: score +10. Otherwise: lose 1 of the cat's seven lives

Have fun!
